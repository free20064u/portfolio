INTRODUCTION
The website is design for a customer to advertise him/herself with respect to what 
he can do when it comes to website design.The website will contain three main pages ie
  Homepage
  Service
  Contact
Each page will have the following components thats 
  Navbar
  Footer

NAVBAR
  The navbar will display the logo and links to the various pages ie
    homepage, service and contact.
    PC VIEW
      The links in the navbar must be visible so that it can be clicked directly but the logo
      should be seen 

    MOBILE VIEW
      The links in the navbar should be hidden however there should be a menu button which 
      will display the links in the navbar in a vertical manner. The background of the should be blared
      when the menu is clicked or touched or when the menu is displayed.

FOOTER
  The footer will contain, the logo, the social media links of the owner it shall also contain the copyright.
  the social media links are twitter, facebook and whatsapp.
    PC
      The logo and the social media links should be displayed in two column layers. the logo on one side and the social media on the other side.
      The copyright should be place at the bottom of the page in the center. It should be placed below the logo and social media links

    MOBILE
      The logo should be hidden, the social media links placed on top of the copyright.
